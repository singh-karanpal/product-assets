USA Soccer Celebration Pack Support

USA Soccer Celebration Pack is a sticker pack for Apple Messages. After installing, open Apple Messages, start or select a conversation, then choose the sticker pack from the Apple Messages app drawer.

Common Help

  • Make sure the app is installed on the same device where you use Apple Messages.
  • Check the Apple Messages app drawer if the sticker pack does not appear immediately.
  • Restart Apple Messages or the device if the sticker pack still does not appear.
  • Confirm that Apple Messages and iOS are up to date.

Contact

Support contact: apps.dev.supports@gmail.com

Response time: Typically within 48 hours.

Privacy

The app does not require an account and does not collect personal data. Read the privacy policy.

Independence

This is an independent fan sticker pack and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any soccer federation, team, league, tournament, or player.
